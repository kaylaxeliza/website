About Me

My name is Kayla Dodson and if you're in search of a creative editor, videographer or photographer, then I'm your gal! I've been editing and practicing filmmaking for around 5 years now and have also picked up a little bit of graphic and web design skills along the way. I graudated from The University of North Carolina at Greensboro with a BA in Media Studies and I'm based in Seattle, WA.

photography

Whether it's simply to capture natural beauty and colors or to completely alter the scene of a photo, I always have my camera ready.
